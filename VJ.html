Vatnajokull

Vatnajokull was created in 2008 and is one of the largest national parks in Europe. Vatnajokull is a large glacier that is thought to have formed over 1000 years ago.

Vatnajokull

The glacier is the largest in Europe. It actually covers some volcanoes in Iceland.
